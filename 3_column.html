Three Column Page

First Idea: Robots as machines to make things

Robots have difficult with simple jobs like walking on 2 legs or patting a dog

Robots can use machine learning to keep practising and lower error rates

Second Idea: Robots as automated cars

Cars only need to speed up or slow down and turn left or turn right

However, they also need proximity sensors and computer vision algorithms

Third Idea: Robots as lethal autonomous weapons

Asimov's laws say Robots must not harm people

Robots will efficiently carry out human orders good or bad
